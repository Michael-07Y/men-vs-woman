Survey Results

This graph serves as a window into the complex landscape of gender dynamics within the workplace, gleaned from comprehensive surveys of work-related experiences. By analyzing responses from students, it offers valuable insights into the multifaceted nature of gender disparities in contemporary work environments.

Finance Details

This graph illustrates the compelling narrative of gender disparities in workplace income over time. By examining the income trends of men and women across various industries, it provides a display in a massive difference of equality by pay.

Education Differences

As time goes on, by the visual of the differences in GPA in college and high school, females have been the leading average for higher GPA. By the visualization, the peak of high school GPA was around 2010 and the lowest drop was around 2020. Also the college graph shows how the Covid pandemic made a massive change to GPA in college by different genders.
